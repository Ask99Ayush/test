# 1. Project Summary

## Carbon Offset Marketplace 2.0

### What is this project?

Carbon Offset Marketplace 2.0 is a next-generation blockchain platform that makes carbon credits transparent, verifiable, and tradeable. It combines three cutting-edge technologies:

- **Aptos Blockchain**: For secure, transparent carbon credit tokenization
- **Artificial Intelligence**: For accurate carbon emission calculations
- **Internet of Things (IoT)**: For real-time environmental impact verification

Think of it as "Amazon for carbon credits" but with blockchain transparency and AI-powered verification.

### The Problem We Solve

Current carbon credit markets have serious issues:

1. **Lack of Transparency**: Companies don't know if credits are real or double-counted
2. **No Real Verification**: Credits are often based on projections, not actual data
3. **Complex Trading**: Buying and selling credits is slow and expensive
4. **Trust Issues**: No way to verify if environmental projects actually work
5. **Poor Tracking**: Credits can be traded multiple times or retired incorrectly
